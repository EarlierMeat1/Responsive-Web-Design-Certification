Here's a timeline of The Doctors life:

  • November 1963 - The first apperance of the "Original" Doctor
  • October 1966 - Regenerates to 2nd Doctor
  • January 1970 - Regenerates to 3rd Doctor
  • June 1974 - Regenerates to 4th Doctor
  • March 1981 - Regenerates to 5th Doctor
  • March 1984 - Regenerates to 6th Doctor
  • September 1987 - Regenerates to 7th Doctor
  • May 1996 - Regenerates to 8th Doctor
  • Date Unknown - Regenerates to the War Doctor
  • March 2005 - Regenerates to 9th Doctor
  • June 2005 - Regenerates to 10th Doctor
  • January 2010 - Regenerates to 11th Doctor
  • December 2013 - Regenerates to 12th Doctor
  • December 2017 - Regenerates to 13th Doctor
